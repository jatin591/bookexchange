About BookExchange

BookExchange is a platform designed to help students share knowledge and resources within their college community. Our mission is to make educational materials more accessible and affordable for everyone.

Founded in 2025, we believe that knowledge should be shared freely. By facilitating book exchanges, donations, and requests, we're building a sustainable ecosystem that benefits both students and the environment.

Our platform connects students across campuses, allowing them to trade textbooks, fiction, non-fiction, and academic resources. Whether you're looking to save money on textbooks or find your next favorite novel, BookExchange makes it easy.
